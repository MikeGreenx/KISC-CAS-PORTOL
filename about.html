About CAS

CAS (Creativity, Action, Service) is a core part of the IB Diploma Programme. It encourages students to engage with their community, develop new skills, and reflect on their learning.

Sample projects

  • Community clean-up and tree planting
  • Peer tutoring and educational clubs
  • Charity drives and fundraisers
“Small actions, big impact.” — Kitengela International CAS
